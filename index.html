// WHY LOCAL

The cloud is someone else's computer.
Yours doesn't have to be.

Modern AI can read, write, sort, and summarize most of the paperwork that slows a business down. But if your client contracts, compliance obligations, or trade secrets say data can't leave your control, the big cloud AI providers are off the table. Vaulterom exists for exactly that gap: serious AI capability, running on a machine in your office.

Compliance without compromise

Healthcare records, defense contracts, student data, legal files — if a regulation or contract governs where your data lives, a local model is the cleanest answer. Nothing is transmitted, so there's nothing to audit on someone else's side.

Your IP stays yours

Designs, formulas, source code, pricing models. Cloud AI terms change; your machine doesn't. Prompts, documents, and outputs never touch a third-party server — and are never anyone's training data.

One machine, predictable cost

No per-token bills, no surprise rate hikes, no usage caps in the middle of a busy week. You buy the hardware once and run it as hard as you want.

// WORKFLOWS

What does it actually do all day?

Workflows are built around your processes, not picked from a menu — but these are the kinds of jobs our systems handle. If a task involves reading, writing, or sorting information, it's probably automatable.

Document intake

Incoming PDFs, scans, and forms read, classified, summarized, and filed automatically.

Contract review

First-pass review that flags unusual clauses, missing terms, and deviations from your standards.

Email triage

Inbound mail sorted by urgency and topic, with draft replies queued for human approval.

Meeting notes

Recordings transcribed on-device, with action items extracted and routed to owners.

Internal Q&A

A private assistant that answers questions from your own manuals, policies, and historical files.

Data extraction

Invoices, purchase orders, and applications turned into clean structured data for your systems.

Report drafting

Recurring status, compliance, and client reports assembled from your data, ready for review.

Support drafts

Customer inquiries answered with drafts grounded in your real product docs and past tickets.
